Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to JSwebart’s Privacy Policy. Your privacy and trust matter deeply to us, and we are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information. This document outlines the types of data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it. Our goal is to provide services that not only meet your needs but also exceed your expectations while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security.

We collect information about our customers and site visitors to enhance your experience, including both identifiable personal information and non-identifiable data. Identifiable personal information is gathered when you interact with our services, such as subscribing, making transactions, or filling out forms on our website. Non-identifiable data is automatically collected when you visit our platforms, ensuring seamless functionality and personalization of our services.

2. Confidentiality

At JSwebart, we prioritize the confidentiality of all personal data entrusted to us. Your information is handled with the utmost care, ensuring it is not shared, sold, or misused for any commercial or marketing purposes. We are committed to maintaining your trust and providing transparency in how your data is used.

3. Collection of Personal Data

    To provide a superior experience, JSwebart collects information through various channels:

  • Website Interactions: Data is collected when you navigate through our websites or those hosted by us.

  • Service Subscriptions: Information provided during sign-ups or service applications.

  • Questionnaires and Feedback Forms: Input from customer surveys and forms.

  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device that enhance user experience by remembering preferences and optimizing performance.

Additionally, we may supplement this information with data from trusted affiliates or partners. While you can disable cookies in your browser settings, this may limit the functionality of certain features on our website.

4. Use of Personal Data

    Your data is used to:

  • Provide customized services and improve your overall experience.

  • Notify you about relevant products, services, or updates.

  • Share anonymized, aggregated insights with partners to improve offerings.

You have the option to opt-out of promotional communications at any time by contacting our support team at +91-9167674367. Rest assured, any shared data is stripped of personal identifiers and is only used for analytical purposes.

5. Activity Records and Device Information

    For administrative and security purposes, we may log:

This data helps us monitor performance and prevent misuse, but it cannot be used to identify you personally.

6. Public Interactions

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. While we strive to partner with reputable entities, JSwebart cannot take responsibility for the privacy practices of external websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

8. Minors

9. Legal Disclosures

JSwebart may revise this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. In case of significant updates, we will notify you through email or a website announcement. We encourage you to periodically review this policy to stay informed.

Your trust fuels our commitment to excellence. If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to us for clarification or support.
